Inventory :

To move a full stack, make sure the area you are moving is free
You can set how many items you can choose using the Quantity section.. You can reset the amount part with the "RESET" button..

Key combination:

  • Left Click: Allows you to drag the item in the selected slot.
  • With Double + Left Mouse Click: Allows you to use the item in the selected slot.
  • If you click on the weapon in your inventory, the equipment button will appear on the screen.
  • With Shift + Left Mouse Click: Moves the item in the selected slot to the other inventory.
  • With GIVE: you can give the nearest player as much as the value you entered in the amount part. "0" returns all..
  • With Craft: You can craft new items by blending required items..

    • Item Durability:

    The strength of the item decreases day by day. The decrease status of the item is not related to using or not using it, it decreases with the elapsed time. Green indicates that the item is in good condition, and red indicates that the item is in poor condition.
